Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get started with Endless Vocals—from joining our community to making the most of bootcamps and coaching.

How do I join Endless Vocals?

  1. Create a free Discord account so you can join our community server.
  2. Set up a Ko-fi account using the same email you plan to use for Discord.
  3. Connect your Discord account to your Ko-fi profile under Settings > Connections. This link is required so Ko-fi can automatically grant you the subscriber role on Discord.
  4. Join the Endless Vocals Discord server.
  5. Subscribe to the Endless Vocals Ko-fi membership to access live bootcamps and replays.

You must complete the Discord → Ko-fi connection before subscribing; otherwise Ko-fi cannot give you access to the member-only Discord channels.

What happens after I connect Discord and Ko-fi?

Once Ko-fi detects your Discord account, the system automatically assigns you the correct roles when your membership is active. Within a few minutes, new private channels will appear where we host bootcamp materials, schedules, and replays. If you don't see the new channels after 15 minutes, disconnect and reconnect the integration from Ko-fi, then rejoin the Discord server.

What do the two-month bootcamps include?

  • Weekly live coaching calls led by Ryan or Tiago (attend live or catch the replay).
  • Step-by-step checklists to keep your practice sessions short and consistent.
  • Feedback threads inside Discord where you can post clips for review.
  • Songwriting, lyric, and DAW mini-lessons woven through the curriculum.

How much does it cost?

Bootcamp access is $24 per month through Ko-fi. The Discord community is free—only the bootcamp membership requires payment. You can start and stop your membership whenever you need.

Who do I contact if I have issues?

Reach out inside the Discord server by tagging @Coach Ryan or @Coach Tiago, or send us a direct message on Ko-fi. We monitor both platforms and will help you get connected.
